# Day 2 Overview

## SC-300 Objective Alignment
- Implement authentication methods and policies.
- Implement Conditional Access and security defaults.
- Implement password protection and authentication resilience controls.

**Note:** Complete Day 1 labs first for reusable test identities.

## Lab Count
- Total labs: 4

## Labs and Estimated Time
1. [Lab 1](lab1.md) - Authentication Methods Policy Baseline
- Accomplishes: configures authentication method policy scope and user targeting.
- Estimated time: 40-60 minutes.

2. [Lab 2](lab2.md) - Self-Service Password Reset (SSPR) and Combined Registration
- Accomplishes: enables and validates SSPR and registration settings.
- Estimated time: 35-55 minutes.

3. [Lab 3](lab3.md) - Conditional Access Baseline Policy
- Accomplishes: creates report-only CA policy and validates impact safely.
- Estimated time: 50-75 minutes.

4. [Lab 4](lab4.md) - Password Protection and Smart Lockout
- Accomplishes: reviews password protection settings and lockout behavior.
- Estimated time: 30-50 minutes.
