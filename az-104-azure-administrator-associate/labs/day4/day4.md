# Day 4 Overview

- Manage Azure identities and governance: policy assignment scope behavior, tag governance, and compliance visibility.
- Monitor and maintain Azure resources: Log Analytics workspace, alerting pipeline, and action group linkage.
- Monitor and maintain Azure resources: Automation Account baseline for repeatable operations.

## Lab Count
- Total labs: 4

1. [Lab 1](lab1.md) - Policy and Tags
- Accomplishes: applies governance tags, assigns built-in tag policy, and validates assignment/compliance behavior.
- Estimated time (new to Azure): 45-65 minutes.

2. [Lab 2](lab2.md) - Monitoring and Alerts
- Accomplishes: builds a minimal monitoring pipeline with Log Analytics, action group, and alert rule.
- Estimated time (new to Azure): 55-80 minutes.

3. [Lab 3](lab3.md) - Automation Starter
- Accomplishes: creates an Automation Account and validates identity and runbook readiness.
- Estimated time (new to Azure): 40-60 minutes.

4. [Lab 4](lab4.md) - Governance Controls
- Accomplishes: applies lock-based guardrails and captures cost/advisor governance details.
- Estimated time (new to Azure): 45-70 minutes.
