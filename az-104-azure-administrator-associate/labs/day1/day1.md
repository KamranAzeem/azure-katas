# Day 1 Overview

## AZ-104 Objective Alignment
- Manage Azure identities and governance: Microsoft Entra identities, group membership, and Azure RBAC scope interpretation. (Governance includes RBAC , Various Policies and Budget)
- Implement and manage storage: secure storage account baseline and storage container readiness.
- Monitor and maintain Azure resources: tag governance signals and Activity Log review at resource group scope.


**Note:** Before you start the labs for Day1, you should complete the prerequisites for this entire course specified in the *main* `README.md` file.

## Lab Count
- Total labs: 4

## Labs and Estimated Time
1. [Lab 1](lab1.md) - RBAC Scope Audit
- Accomplishes: verifies direct vs inherited access at `rg-az104` and documents effective role scope.
- Estimated time (new to Azure): 50-70 minutes.

2. [Lab 2](lab2.md) - Storage Baseline
- Accomplishes: deploys a baseline storage account with secure defaults and validates basic data protection readiness.
- Estimated time (new to Azure): 45-65 minutes.

3. [Lab 3](lab3.md) - Governance and Monitoring Starter
- Accomplishes: applies and validates governance tags, then captures operational details from Activity Log.
- Estimated time (new to Azure): 40-60 minutes.

4. [Lab 4](lab4.md) - Identity Governance Extras
- Accomplishes: validates users/groups/guests and maps identity administration to RBAC authorization scope.
- Estimated time (new to Azure): 50-75 minutes.
